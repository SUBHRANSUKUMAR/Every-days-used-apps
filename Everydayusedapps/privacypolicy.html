Privacy Policy

App Name: Mini Calculator

Last Updated:

Thank you for using Mini Calculator. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

Mini Calculator is a calculator application designed to perform standard calculations with the ability to save and view history. We are committed to respecting your privacy.

2. Information Collection and Use

We believe in complete privacy. Therefore:

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not store any user data on external servers.
  • We do not transmit any data to third parties.

The application operates completely offline.

3. App Permissions

Mini Calculator requires no special permissions to run. We do not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or external storage files unrelated to the app's core function.

4. Data Storage

The "History" feature allows you to save and view past calculations. This data is stored locally on your device within the application's private storage. If you uninstall the application or clear the app data, this history will be deleted. We have no access to this data.

5. Third-Party Services

Since the app is offline and free, we do not use third-party analytics services or advertising networks that track user behavior.

6. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, our application is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
